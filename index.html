About the Club

More than a club — a community building future explorers.

The Space and Aviation Club at Prince Sultan University is a student-led community dedicated to exploring the world of aerospace through hands-on experiences, events, and knowledge sharing.

Through workshops, industry talks, and interactive activities, we aim to bridge the gap between passion and real-world application, empowering students to discover opportunities in aviation and space beyond the classroom.
