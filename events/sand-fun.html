Location

Category

Public Engagement Event

About the Event

Sand & Fun was a public engagement event where the Space & Aviation Club represented Prince Sultan University through a dedicated interactive booth. The club introduced visitors to the Aviation Management program and shared insights about aviation in an engaging and approachable way.

Throughout the event, club members actively interacted with visitors, answering questions and providing a clear view of aviation opportunities and academic pathways at the university.

The event also marked a special moment as the team had the opportunity to meet His Royal Highness Prince Sultan bin Salman, reflecting the significance of the club’s participation.

In recognition of their contribution, the club received a certificate of appreciation for its active involvement and representation.
