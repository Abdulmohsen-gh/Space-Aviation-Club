Global Airport Forum

About the Event

Airport Forum was a major aviation event where the Space & Aviation Club represented Prince Sultan University in a professional industry setting.

Building on previous experiences, the club delivered an interactive presence similar to its participation in Sand & Fun, engaging with visitors and contributing to discussions around aviation and airport development.

Throughout the event, members connected with professionals, explored key industry topics, and gained deeper insight into the aviation sector beyond the academic environment.

In recognition of the club’s efforts and impactful participation, Prince Sultan University was honored at the end of the forum — reflecting the team’s commitment and contribution.

Recognition: Prince Sultan University was honored for its participation in the forum.
